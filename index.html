How to Make a Business Card on Adobe Illustrator...

Assembly time: 30 minutes
Awesome rating: 80% awesome

Business cards are an effective way to advertise yourself! Follow this simple tutorial to learn to create your own business card using Adobe Illustrator.

  • Things You'll Need

  • A computer
  • Internet access
  • Adobe Illustrator (there is a 7 day free trail)
  • Patience

  1. >Go to Adobe Illustrator and create a 2 x 3.5 inch template for your business card. Create a bleed and set all sides to 0.125 inch.

  2. Set up your document color mode to CMYK.

    To do this you can go to File > Document Color Mode > CMYK.

  3. Create guides for your business card.

    To do this hold down command > R. Go up to the top ruler and drag down a horizontal line to an eighth of an inch. Go to the side ruler and drag a vertical line to an eighth of an inch. Repeat that for the other 2 sides.(Check the image below to see where the lines fall at.)

  4. Add a white rectangle.

    Click the rectangle tool and create a white rectangle the same size as the bleed.

  5. Design your card!

    A successful business card includes important information like you name or the name of your business and contact information. Her are some ideas of effective business cards.

  6. Choose all of your text and go to the Type > Create outline.

    This step can change your type to be a vector, allowing you to open your file in another program without losing the font.

  7. Print copies of your card so you can see if your text is too small or too big.

    Save your work as an AI file and then save the new file as an EPS file. Your file is now ready to print out!
